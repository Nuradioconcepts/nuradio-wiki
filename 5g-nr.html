5G NR 🚧

5G NR

5G New Radio

5G NR (New Radio) is the global standard for the 5G air interface, defined by 3GPP. This section covers system architecture, the air interface, data encapsulation, and procedures organized by network component.

Topics​

🏗️System Architecture 🚧NG-RAN, 5G Core, control-plane and user-plane decomposition, and key network functions.📶Air Interface 🚧Frame structure, time and frequency domains, numerologies, resource blocks, and synchronization signals.🗂️Data Encapsulation 🚧Protocol stack, channel types, logical/transport/physical channel mappings, and interfaces.⚙️Procedures 🚧Layer and interface procedures organized by network component — UE, NG-RAN, 5G Core, and 5G System.
